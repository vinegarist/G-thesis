深度神经网络在图像分类等任务中取得了优异性能，但其对对抗样本的高度敏感引发了安全性担忧。现有对抗性训练方法主要针对$L_p$范数约束下的梯度攻击进行防御，对遮蔽类攻击的研究相对匮乏。本文围绕基于特征归因的遮蔽攻击及其对抗性训练方法展开研究。首先实现了基于原始梯度显著性图与积分梯度两种归因方法的遮蔽攻击，并分别设计了固定遮蔽与自适应遮蔽两种攻击策略；进而提出了将遮蔽攻击与PGD梯度攻击相结合的混合对抗性训练方法（Mix-AT）。在MNIST数据集与LeNet-5模型上完成了九种防御策略与七类攻击的系统实验。结果表明：自适应遮蔽攻击的攻击强度可超越PGD与C\&W等主流梯度攻击；仅以单一类型攻击训练的模型对训练外攻击防御能力严重退化；Mix-AT在干净准确率、梯度攻击防御与遮蔽攻击防御之间取得了良好平衡，其中"自适应积分梯度遮蔽+PGD"组合综合表现最优；迁移攻击实验进一步揭示了部分纯遮蔽训练模型存在严重的梯度掩蔽现象。

}

\begin{abstract}
\begin{spacing}{1.5}
Deep neural networks have achieved remarkable performance in tasks such as image classification, yet their vulnerability to adversarial examples raises serious security concerns. Most existing adversarial training methods focus on gradient-based attacks under $L_p$-norm constraints, while defenses against occlusion attacks remain relatively underexplored. This thesis investigates feature attribution-based occlusion attacks and the corresponding adversarial training methods. We first implement occlusion attacks built on two attribution methods—gradient saliency maps and Integrated Gradients—and design both fixed and adaptive occlusion strategies. We then propose a mixed adversarial training method (Mix-AT) that combines occlusion attacks with PGD gradient attacks. Systematic experiments are conducted on the MNIST dataset with LeNet-5, evaluating nine defense strategies against seven types of attacks. The results show that adaptive occlusion attacks can surpass mainstream gradient attacks such as PGD and C\&W in attack strength; models trained with a single type of attack suffer severe degradation against unseen attacks; Mix-AT achieves a favorable balance among clean accuracy, robustness to gradient attacks, and robustness to occlusion attacks, with the ``adaptive Integrated-Gradients occlusion + PGD'' combination performing best overall; transfer-attack experiments further reveal severe gradient masking in some occlusion-only adversarially trained models.

\end{spacing}
\end{abstract}
