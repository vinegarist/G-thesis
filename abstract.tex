深度神经网络在图像分类等任务中取得了优异性能，但其对对抗样本的高度敏感引发了安全性担忧。现有对抗性训练方法主要针对$L_p$范数约束下的梯度攻击进行防御，对遮蔽类攻击的防御研究相对匮乏。本文围绕基于显著性图的遮蔽攻击及其对抗性训练方法展开研究。首先，实现了两种特征归因方法——梯度显著性图与梯度积分，分别设计了固定遮蔽攻击和自适应遮蔽攻击两种攻击变体，并系统对比了两种归因方法在攻击效果与计算效率上的差异。在MNIST数据集上基于LeNet-5模型进行了系统实验。结果表明，梯度积分遮蔽攻击在攻击强度上优于显著性遮蔽攻击，但计算代价显著更高；基于显著性遮蔽的对抗性训练可有效提升模型对遮蔽攻击的防御能力；混合对抗性训练策略实现了梯度攻击防御与遮蔽攻击防御的较好平衡。

}

\end{spacing}

\begin{abstract}
\begin{spacing}{1.5}
Deep neural networks have achieved excellent performance in tasks such as image classification, but their high sensitivity to adversarial examples has raised security concerns. Existing adversarial training methods mainly focus on defending against $L_p$-norm constrained gradient attacks, while research on defense against occlusion-based attacks remains relatively scarce. This thesis investigates saliency map-based occlusion attacks and their corresponding adversarial training methods. First, two feature attribution methods—gradient saliency map and Integrated Gradients—are implemented, with both fixed occlusion attack and adaptive occlusion attack designed as attack variants. The two attribution methods are systematically compared in terms of attack effectiveness and computational efficiency. Systematic experiments are conducted on the MNIST dataset using LeNet-5 as the base model. Results show that Integrated Gradients-based occlusion attacks outperform saliency-based attacks in attack strength but with significantly higher computational cost; saliency occlusion-based adversarial training effectively improves model defense against occlusion attacks; the mixed adversarial training strategy achieves a good balance between gradient attack defense and occlusion attack defense.

\end{spacing}
\end{abstract}
